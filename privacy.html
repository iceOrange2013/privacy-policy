HiBoyS2 App

Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the HiBoyS2 app. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring transparent handling of your data.

1. Privacy Protection

1.1 This app will not disclose or provide any individual user's personal information or usage data to third parties, except in the following cases:

  • (1) Explicit authorization has been obtained from the user;
  • (2) As required by laws and regulations;
  • (3) As requested by relevant government departments;
  • (4) To safeguard public interests or legitimate rights.

1.2 When cooperating with third parties to provide relevant functions, if the third party agrees to assume the same user privacy protection responsibilities as this app, this app may provide information to them within the necessary scope to realize the functions, without separately notifying the user.

1.3 Without disclosing users' personal information, this app may conduct anonymous statistics and analysis on user data and use it for legitimate purposes such as product optimization.

2. Agreement Modifications

2.1 This app reserves the right to modify this privacy policy when necessary. After the policy is changed, users will be notified through in-app announcements or page updates.

2.2 If users do not agree with the modified content, they may choose to stop using this app. Continued use will be deemed as acceptance of the modified privacy policy terms.

3. App Permissions Explanation

3.1 Bluetooth Permission

  • (1) This app needs to use Bluetooth permission to connect to the HiBoyS2 device, enabling device control and data interaction.
  • (2) This permission is only used for device connection-related functions and not for collecting or storing any user information.

3.2 Location Permission

  • (1) Since Android and iOS systems require location permission for Bluetooth scanning, the app may request location permission to implement Bluetooth functions.
  • (2) This app will not collect or use your actual location information; this permission is only required for system compatibility.

3.3 Network Access Permission

  • (1) This app uses network access permission for functions such as checking device firmware versions and obtaining update information.
  • (2) All network-connected behaviors are only for function implementation and will not upload user privacy information or share it with third parties.
